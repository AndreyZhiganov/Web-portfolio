ABOUT KEY PROJECTS
Performance-based Interdisciplinary Artist. His practice was forged at the Stanislavsky Electrotheatre — a venue that became the epicenter of avant-garde music and performance art in Russia during the 2020s. He works not with music but with sound, not with image but with texture and form, prioritizing a physical rather than a conceptual impact on the viewer. His work is a pursuit of absolute sincerity: expressing peaks of emotion through abstract imagery, creating unique experiences that expand the viewer's sensibility.

This pursuit leads him to forms where human presence, music, visual imagery, and text exist equally, in open dialogue. By crystallizing fragments of reality on stage, interweaving art forms, and engaging contemporary philosophy and theory, he creates events capable of influencing not just audiences, but the entire world — genuinely believing that art can achieve this.
